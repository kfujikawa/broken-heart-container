Breakups are hard

Whether you're the dumper or the dumpee; breakups are never easy. That's why, we've created an integration with Spotify to bring you a list organized by valence. Valence, as used in psychology, is the intrinsic attractiveness (positive valence) or aversiveness (negative valence) of some event or object (breakup songs in our case!)

Heart Container Rating System

Rating: Over it

Congratulations; you have used the power of music to heal your wounded soul and are ready to get back out there.

Rating: Healing

You aren't quite ready to date, but have started trolling Tinder and flirted with that cute Barista. Hang in there brave warrior and advance to the next level.

Rating: Coping

You still wake up expecting them to be there, but alas, you're still sleeping on a friends couch and haven't changed your clothes for three days. Select some Healing or Over it selections to pump up your mood

Wallowing

You've started seeing someone, but unfortunately, that someone is Ben and Jerry. Your chest is a vacant hole where your heart used to lie before they ripped it out.
